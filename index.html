RuLu Ruby Conference

A two-day conference dedicated to Ruby

19-20th June 2014 | Lyon, France

Ruby Lugdunum (aka RuLu) is a two-day conference dedicated to exploring the Ruby language and basking in warmth of the amazing Ruby community. Last edition of RuLu took place in Lyon, France on June 19-20th 2014.
